FAQ

      
Fast answers for recruiters, teammates, and graders.


      

        

          What makes ApplyMate different from “rewrite my CV” tools?
          
It blends the JD with market-level role expectations (skills common across many postings) and shows an Agent Trace for transparency.

        


        

          Can I see what the agent actually did?
          
Yes—each run outputs trace steps (prompt/response) so you can audit and understand the reasoning.

        


        

          What happens after rejection?
          
ApplyMate proposes concrete fixes: bullet upgrades, missing skill coverage, and an action plan to re-apply stronger.

        


        

          What happens after approval / interview invite?
          
It generates an interview prep pack: likely questions, talking points, and stories aligned to your tailored CV.
